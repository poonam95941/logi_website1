About Mass Express Cargo

From the vibrant cities to the remotest corners of Maharashtra, Goa, and Gujarat, Mass Express Cargo (MEC) ensures every shipment reaches its destination — swiftly, safely, and seamlessly.

Founded by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Kishor Mutha, MEC began with a mission to redefine logistics in India. What started as a bold vision has grown into a legacy built on innovation, trust, and performance.

With over 18 years of expertise in express distribution and supply chain management, MEC continues to lead with technology-driven operations, customer-first service, and a commitment to excellence.

At MEC, every route we plan and every delivery we make is guided by one goal — to connect possibilities, empower businesses, and move India forward.
